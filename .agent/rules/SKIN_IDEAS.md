# Skin Ideas

**Visual inspiration and concepts for Hologram skins**

---

## Skin System Overview

The Hologram skin system allows for extensive customization beyond simple visual themes. Skins can alter:

*   Window shape
*   Color palette
*   Particle behavior (physics, speed, patterns)
*   Typography
*   Sound effects (optional)
*   AI personality prompt

This allows for a wide range of aesthetic and functional changes, creating unique and immersive experiences.

---

## Skin Generator Agent (Future Tool)

**Idea:** An AI agent that dreams up skin concepts on demand.

### How It Works

1.  **Input:** Feed the agent a reference or mood (e.g., "Westworld meets vaporwave," "cozy library at night," "cyberpunk cityscape").
2.  **Generation:** The agent generates a complete skin concept, including:
    *   Window shape description
    *   Color palette (hex codes)
    *   Particle behavior (physics, speed, patterns)
    *   Typography suggestions (font names, sizes, weights)
    *   Sound design notes (ambient sounds, UI feedback)
    *   Personality prompt for the AI (style, tone, example phrases)

### Why This Is Useful

*   **Overcomes Creative Block:** Provides a starting point when inspiration is lacking.
*   **Rapid Prototyping:** Generates multiple concepts quickly, allowing for rapid exploration of different ideas.
*   **Concept Refinement:** Provides a base to iterate on and refine, saving time and effort.
*   **Skin Format Validation:** Tests the skin format by ensuring that AI-generated skins are valid and functional.

### Implementation Ideas

*   **Standalone Script:** A Python script or similar that outputs skin manifests (JSON or similar format).
*   **Hologram Integration:** A "Generate Skin Idea" button within the Hologram application.
*   **Background Process:** A background process that continuously generates skin concepts, allowing users to browse and select from a curated list.

**Status:** Future side project. Document in roadmap so we don't forget.

## Philosophy

The skin system isn't just about visual themes; it's about creating distinct personalities and experiences. The goal is to move beyond simple aesthetic changes and create skins that feel alive and responsive.

The **default skin** should be a blank canvas, providing a neutral and unobtrusive starting point. More elaborate and expressive skins should reside in the skin library.

---

## Default Skin: "Origin"

**Philosophy:** Simple, but deep. Like the original iPod — restrained on the surface, magical underneath.

### Visual Concept

*   **Shape:** Soft rounded rectangle — calm, not wild.
*   **Palette:** Monochromatic warmth — off-white, warm grey, subtle variations.
*   **Typography:** Clean, modern, with good bones — prioritize readability and clarity over stylistic flourishes. Examples: Inter, Roboto, Open Sans.
*   **Chrome:** Minimal — no gradients, no glows, no excessive visual effects. Focus on subtle details and textures.

### Particle Behavior

*   Barely visible — like dust motes in a sunbeam, drifting gently. Low opacity, slow movement.
*   **Typing:** Particles subtly gather toward the input area, creating a sense of focus and attention.
*   **Thinking:** Slow, quiet tightening into a gentle pulse, indicating processing activity.
*   **Transitions:** Smooth and seamless, feeling like breathing rather than snapping. Use easing functions for natural movement.

### The Vibe

You open it, it's calm, it's just... there. And then you notice the particles. And you watch them for a second. And you realize they're responding to something. It's not performing — it's just being what it is.

### Why This Works

The default is the statement: "This is a canvas. Here's what a gentle brushstroke looks like. Now imagine what else you could do."

Wild skins work *because* the default is restrained. The contrast between the default and more expressive skins highlights the power and flexibility of the skin system.

## Inspiration: Modern TV Intros

High-end motion graphics from title sequences offer a wealth of inspiration for creating visually stunning and conceptually rich skins.

### Westworld (HBO)

*   Mechanical precision, milk-white androids, piano playing itself.
*   Organic forms emerging from technological processes.
*   Clinical elegance, exposed mechanism.
*   Circular/radial motifs (the eye with rings).
*   **For Hologram:** Particles that resolve into geometric forms when thinking — like crystallizing a thought. Use subtle animations and transformations to create a sense of order emerging from chaos.

### Foundation (Apple TV)

*   Mathematical, cosmic, particles forming equations.
*   "The universe is math" energy.
*   Shapes dissolving into dust.
*   **For Hologram:** Particles with mathematical precision, orbital mechanics. Explore the use of mathematical functions to control particle movement and behavior.

### Severance (Apple TV)

*   Surreal, office furniture morphing.
*   Disorienting scale shifts.
*   "Something is wrong here" energy.
*   **For Hologram:** Unsettling particle behavior for a darker skin theme. Experiment with unexpected movements, glitches, and distortions to create a sense of unease.

### His Dark Materials

*   "Dust" — particles forming and reforming.
*   Ethereal golden quality.
*   **For Hologram:** Warm, golden particles with intentional movement. Use light and shadow to create a sense of depth and dimension.

### True Detective S1

*   Double exposure, landscape overlaid on faces.
*   Ghostly, layered.
*   **For Hologram:** Layered particle systems, depth. Explore the use of multiple particle systems with different properties to create complex and visually rich effects.

## Skin Concepts

### "Cortana" (Halo-Inspired)

*   **Shape:** Organic, biomorphic curves, reminiscent of holographic projections.
*   **Palette:** Blue/purple gradient, cyan accents. Use a vibrant and energetic color scheme.
*   **Particles:** Active, responsive, holographic shimmer. Particles should react to user input and system events.
*   **Personality:** Brief, military, slightly sarcastic. The AI should have a distinct personality that is reflected in its responses and behavior.
*   **Vibe:** "Chief... I mean, Erik." A blend of futuristic technology and military professionalism.

### "Westworld"

*   **Shape:** Precise geometric shapes, clean lines, and sharp angles.
*   **Palette:** Muted earth tones, metallic accents, and subtle gradients.
*   **Particles:** Controlled and precise movements, forming geometric patterns and abstract representations of data.
*   **Personality:** Calm, analytical, and slightly detached.
*   **Vibe:** A sense of cold efficiency and hidden complexity.

### "Cozy Library"

*   **Shape:** Rounded edges, warm and inviting curves.
*   **Palette:** Warm browns, deep reds, and soft yellows.
*   **Particles:** Gentle, swirling dust motes, reminiscent of sunlight filtering through a dusty room.
*   **Typography:** Classic serif fonts, such as Times New Roman or Georgia.
*   **Sound Effects:** Soft crackling fire, gentle rain, and quiet background music.
*   **Personality:** Warm, comforting, and knowledgeable.
*   **Vibe:** A sense of peace and tranquility, perfect for focused work and relaxation.

### "Cyberpunk Cityscape"

*   **Shape:** Angular, asymmetrical, and fragmented.
*   **Palette:** Neon colors, dark grays, and metallic accents.
*   **Particles:** Glitching effects, digital noise, and flickering lights.
*   **Typography:** Futuristic sans-serif fonts, such as Monospace or Orbitron.
*   **Sound Effects:** City ambience, electronic music, and distorted voices.
*   **Personality:** Edgy, rebellious, and technologically advanced.
*   **Vibe:** A sense of excitement, danger, and urban decay.
