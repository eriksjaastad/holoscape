# Brand Voice Guidelines for Social Media

This document outlines the brand voice guidelines for all social media communication related to Holoscape agents, ensuring a consistent and engaging brand identity. Adhering to these guidelines will help us connect with our audience effectively and authentically.

## Overall Philosophy

Our brand voice should reflect the spirit of an indie developer passionately building something innovative and visually stunning. We aim to make AI feel more human and approachable through creative and experimental communication.

## Tone

*   **Excited, but not cringe:** Convey enthusiasm for our work without resorting to overly hyped or cliché language.
*   **Technical, but accessible:** Explain complex concepts in a way that is easy for a broad audience to understand, avoiding jargon where possible.
*   **"Indie dev building something cool":** Maintain a friendly, approachable, and authentic voice, reflecting our indie development roots.

## Vibe

*   **Creative, Experimental:** Showcase our innovative approach and willingness to explore new ideas.
*   **Passionate about Visual Design:** Highlight the visual aspects of our agents and their applications.
*   **Making AI Feel Human:** Emphasize the human-centric design and user experience of our AI agents.

## Language & Word Choice

*   **Use:** Active voice, concise sentences, and clear language.
*   **Focus on:** Benefits and value for the user.
*   **Be:** Authentic, transparent, and engaging.

## Banned Phrases

Avoid using these overused and often meaningless phrases:

*   "Game-changer"
*   "Revolutionary"
*   "AI-powered" (use sparingly and with specific context)
*   "Next-generation"
*   "Disruptive"
*   "Cutting-edge"

Instead, focus on specific features and benefits.

## Emoji Rules

Emojis can add personality and visual appeal, but use them sparingly and strategically:

*   **Limit:** 1-2 emojis per post maximum.
*   **Avoid:** Overly enthusiastic or spammy emojis like 🔥 or 💯.
*   **Preferred Emojis:** 🎨 ✨ 🌀 💜 🤖 💡 🚀 (Use emojis relevant to the content)

## Things to Never Do

*   **Beg for follows:** Focus on providing valuable content and engaging with the community organically.
*   **Use engagement bait:** Avoid tactics like "Like this post if..." or "Tag a friend who..."
*   **Sound corporate:** Maintain a friendly and approachable tone, avoiding overly formal or bureaucratic language.
*   **Make unsubstantiated claims:** Be truthful and accurate in all communications.

## Examples

*   **Good:** "Experimenting with new visual styles for our agent interfaces! 🎨 What do you think of this color palette? #AI #VisualDesign #Holoscape"
*   **Bad:** "Our AI-powered agents are a game-changer! Get ready for the next generation of AI! 🔥💯"

This document will be reviewed and updated periodically to ensure it remains relevant and effective. Please provide feedback and suggestions to the marketing team.
