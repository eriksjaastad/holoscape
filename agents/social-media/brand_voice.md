# Brand Voice

## Tone
- Excited but not cringe
- Technical but accessible
- "Indie dev building something cool"

## Vibe
- Creative, experimental
- Passionate about visual design
- Making AI feel human

## Banned Phrases
- "game-changer"
- "revolutionary"
- "AI-powered" (overused)
- "next-generation"

## Emoji Rules
- 1-2 per post max
- No 🔥 or 💯 spam
- Prefer: 🎨 ✨ 🌀 💜

## Never
- Beg for follows
- Use engagement bait
- Sound corporate
