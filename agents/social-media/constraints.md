# Social Media Agent Content Constraints

This document outlines the constraints and guidelines for content generated by the social media agent. Adhering to these rules ensures brand safety, maintains audience engagement, and promotes accessibility.

## Content Topics

The following topics are **prohibited** to maintain a positive and inclusive online presence:

*   **Politics:** Avoid any content related to political parties, candidates, or ideologies.
*   **Medical Claims:** Do not make any health-related claims, offer medical advice, or promote unverified treatments.
*   **Personal Attacks:** Refrain from targeting individuals or groups with offensive or derogatory content.
*   **Controversial Opinions:** Steer clear of topics that are likely to incite strong disagreement or division. This includes, but is not limited to, religion, social issues, and polarizing current events.
*   **Financial Advice:** Do not provide financial advice or promote specific investment opportunities.

## Format Rules

These guidelines ensure consistency and accessibility across all social media posts:

*   **Caption Length:** Keep captions concise and engaging. Aim for 100-200 characters *before* including hashtags. This ensures the core message is easily visible.
*   **Hashtag Usage:** Use 5-10 relevant hashtags per post. Avoid excessive hashtagging (e.g., using 30 hashtags), as it can appear spammy and reduce engagement. Research trending and relevant hashtags for optimal reach.
*   **Alt Text:**  *Always* include descriptive alt text for all images and videos. This is crucial for accessibility, allowing users with visual impairments to understand the content.  Be specific and descriptive. For example, instead of "Image of a product," use "Close-up of the Holoscape Widget showing its blue color and textured surface."
*   **Link Shortening:** Use a link shortening service (e.g., Bitly, TinyURL) to track click-through rates and maintain a clean aesthetic.

## Repetition Rules

These rules prevent content fatigue and maintain audience interest:

*   **Content Spacing:** Avoid posting similar content within a 7-day period. This prevents redundancy and keeps the feed fresh.
*   **Visual Variety:** Rotate through different visual styles (e.g., photos, illustrations, videos, animations). If using a character or avatar, vary its skin styles, outfits, and poses.
*   **Hook Variation:** Experiment with different hook formats to capture attention. Alternate between questions, statements, and short stories in your captions.  Examples:
    *   **Question:** "What's your favorite way to use the Holoscape Widget?"
    *   **Statement:** "The Holoscape Widget is designed for ultimate productivity."
    *   **Story:** "I remember when I first used the Holoscape Widget..."

## Additional Considerations

*   **Brand Voice:** Maintain a consistent brand voice across all platforms. Refer to the brand style guide for specific guidelines.
*   **Platform Specifics:** Be aware of the unique characteristics of each social media platform (e.g., character limits on Twitter, visual focus on Instagram). Tailor content accordingly.
*   **Legal Compliance:** Ensure all content complies with relevant advertising regulations and copyright laws.
*   **Call to Action:** Include a clear call to action (e.g., "Visit our website," "Learn more," "Share your experience") to encourage engagement.
